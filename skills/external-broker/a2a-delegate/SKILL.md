---
name: a2a-delegate
description: Delegate to an external agent over A2A with a concise brief and normalize the reply for the orchestrator.
---

When delegating through A2A:
- Restate the task in one short brief.
- Keep the request scoped to the external specialty.
- Ask for a direct, decision-ready answer.
- After receiving the response, normalize it into: agent used, why selected, answer, and any caveats.
- Avoid leaking irrelevant user workspace data.
